• Ground Floor Hall: Polished marble or marble-finish tiles
• First Floor Hall & Corridor: Polished marble (white/cream)

• Bathrooms & Kitchen: Non-slip ceramic tiles
• Terrace: Vitrified outdoor tiles

WALLS:
• External: 9" brick with travertine stone cladding
• Internal: 4.5" brick with plaster + paint/tiles
• Detailing: Stainless steel/bronze accents

DOORS & WINDOWS:
• Material: Teak-ply with melamine laminate
• Glazing: 6mm clear (internal), 10mm tinted (external)
• Locks: Mortise or cylindrical (Godrej/Dorset)

LIGHTING:
• LED tubes (4000K task), LED panels (3000K ambient)
• Warm chandeliers (2700K focal)
• Cove lights in hall (2700K)
• Optional fiber-optic starry ceiling

HVAC:
• Ground Floor: Large central AC system (adaptable)
• First Floor Hall: 2.5 Ton AC
• Master Bed: 1.5 Ton AC
• Guest: 0.75 Ton AC
• Secondary Bed: 1.5 Ton AC
• Bathroom exhaust: 200 CFM each
• Kitchen chimney: 600mm, ducted

MEP:
• Water: Central 110mm main → distribution through duct
